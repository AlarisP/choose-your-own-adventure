About the project

A Cave Outside of Time

Edward Packard's The Cave of Time (1979) was the first book in the Choose Your Own Adventure® series. A single story fractures across 111 pages into 45 distinct endings — from the Ice Age to the year 3742.

This project OCR-extracts every page, builds a directed graph of all choices, and lets you read any path or explore the structure visually. The pipeline is fully reproducible from the original PDF.
